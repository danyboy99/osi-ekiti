Welcome to Osi-ekiti

Osi-ekiti is a traditional town in the Osi Local Government Area of Ekiti State, Nigeria. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and scenic beauty. The town is located in the heart of the Ekiti Hills, surrounded by lush greenery and picturesque landscapes.
